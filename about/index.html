About The MA.R.I.A. Files

mariafiles.com aims to be the definitive and comprehensive repository of all scientific literature on the subject of medical masking.

MA.R.I.A. (Mask Research Institute of America) is an alliance of physicians who practice medicine in the Americas, and who have chosen to remain anonymous.

Our anonymity does not detract from the credibility of this website, because we are not herein publishing our own studies, but are instead curating, hosting, and distributing studies that have been published by others.

Our official position on mandatory universal public masking:

In light of the overwhelming body of evidence presented on this site which demonstrates, not only the ineffectiveness of masks to stop the transmission of virus aerosols, but, also, the undeniable harmful health effects caused by wearing a mask, we can only conclude that the government enforcement of mandatory universal masking is not about public health, but about something else entirely.

Our Patrons:

Our principal patroness and guardian is The Immaculata. This site is also commended to the patronage and protection of The Beloved Physician.
